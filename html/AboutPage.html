Jalil Naji PhD, PEng

Parhoon Canada Inc. was established in toronto, Ontario by Jalil Naji in 2016. Jalil holds a PhD in mechanical engineering. He has been integrating academic knowledge, intellectual curiosity, and industry experience to upgrade engineering systems in field of vibration, noise control, reliability, and condition monitoring. Wide vibration knowledge and experimental vibration test, modal analysis on metals, composites, viscoelastic materials and smart materials (MRF) led him to be an outstanding expert in this field.

Publications:
