About Our CEO:

Parhonn canada Inc. was established in 2016 in Toronto, Ontario. Jalil Naji is founder and CEO. Jalil combined academic knowledge, intellectual curiosity, and industry/Lab experience to upgrade engineering systems in field of vibration, reliability, test and condition monitoring. Wide vibration knowledge and experimental vibration/modal test on composite, viscoelastic and innovative smart materials led him to be an outstanding expert in this filed.

Jalil Naji PhD, PEng
