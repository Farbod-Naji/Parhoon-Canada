Parhoon Canada Inc

Vibration is Awareness

Vibration Analysis, Modal Analysis, Vibrational Monitoring, Noise Control, Reliability

Parhoon Canada Inc. is dedicated to practising and consulting in the disciplines of vibration analysis, modal analysis, vibration based condition monitoring, noise control and reliability.

We are experts in modal analysis of structure and vibration analysis of rotatory equipment for many years. Our competency is to analyze vibration signals and to distinguish vibrational signatures so as to locate all sources of vibration at one time.

We employ wide range of methods, based on vibration analysis in all three phases of machine condition monitoring, namely fault detection, fault diagnosis and fault prognosis (prediction of remaining useful life).
